📖 How to get your Instagram Data? ▼
  1. Go to Settings & Activity on your Instagram App or Website.
  2. Select Accounts Center > Your information and permissions.
  3. Click Download your information > Download or transfer information.
  4. Choose Some of your information (to save time).
  5. Scroll down and check Followers and following. Click Next.
  6. CRITICAL SETTINGS:
    • Change Format to: JSON
    • Change Date Range to: All Time
  7. Click Create Files. Instagram will email you when the .zip is ready!
